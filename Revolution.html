Revolution of Internet

South Korea’s journey from early broadband to nationwide 5G is one of the fastest digital transformations in the world.

Overview of South Korea Internet

South Korea has been a global leader in internet connectivity since the 1990s, with high-speed broadband infrastructure, widespread adoption of mobile internet, and innovative online services. This foundation has helped the country achieve a hyperconnected society, transforming businesses, education, and daily life.

Source: Arirang News - South Korea's ICT Development (YouTube, 2018).

Founding Father of South Korea’s Internet

Dr. Kilnam Chon

Known as the “Father of the Korean Internet,” Dr. Kilnam Chon pioneered the development of computer networking in South Korea. He played a crucial role in connecting South Korea to the global internet, laying the foundation for the nation’s rapid digital transformation.

Early Networking

Developed the first Korean internet connection in the 1980s, helping South Korea join the global digital community.

Academic Leader

Professor at KAIST, where he trained the next generation of computer scientists and innovators in South Korea.

Global Internet

Played a key role in expanding internet adoption in Asia and contributed to international internet governance discussions.

Recognitions

Awarded multiple honors for his contribution to technology, including recognition from the Internet Society (ISOC).
