The Korean Wave: From Domestic Technology to Global Culture

To achieve unprecedented global penetration, the seamless integration of high-speed internet and digital platforms was important for disseminating Korean cultural content.

Welcome to the Korean Wave

The Korean Wave, or Hallyu, refers to the global popularity of South Korean culture, including K-pop, K-dramas, and Korean cuisine. This phenomenon has been fueled by the country's advanced digital infrastructure and the widespread use of social media platforms, allowing for the rapid dissemination of cultural content worldwide.
